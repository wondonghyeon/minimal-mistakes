About

I am an M.S. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA). Currently, I am working with Professor Jungseock Joo. My research interest is computer vision, human-A.I interaction, image captioning and social media analysis.
I received my B.S. in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Seoul National University (SNU).
